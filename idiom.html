Salty Glutton
// Jena Jekums
Belly down on my board-
Like a drunk-
Doubled over laughing,
Bursting at the seams.
Because the ocean got me
Once again.
In my ecstasy,
a tear rolled down
My neck. My
Mouth went to get it back;
drank the whole ocean instead.
